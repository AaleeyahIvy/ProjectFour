Aropia was founded in 2021 by Aaron & Abagail Piach. They were discovering islands and small land masses near Japangea, this is when they stumbled across a large island uninhabited. The Piach's made the decision to create a public community and allow others to reside in the island. The official language is English, but most Japangeans live here.
