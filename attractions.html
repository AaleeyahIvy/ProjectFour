Food

The infamous Coral Shack featuring the Deap Sea Burger and Coral Fries is the ultimate favorite for tourists. Rated 5/5 stars everywhere!

Adventure

Aropia has 3 tall mountains that are exciting to climb and explore. Pristine clear streams and rivers flow downward making every step tranquil. There is a Raging Waters Waterpark, Ferocious Ferry facility and Event center with all-year round occasions to attend.

Events

Every year, there is a city-wide cosplay convention. Most of the community dresses up as their favorite characters from any show, movie, anime, comic etc. This event is held on May 23rd
